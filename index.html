Neem

Scientific Name:

Azadirachta Indica

Family:

Meliaceae

Temperature in Celcius:

20-45

Neem(Azadirachta indica) is a fast-growing, evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent, widely recognized for its remarkable medicinal properties and environmental benefits. Standing tall with a height that can reach up to 15-30 meters (50-98 feet), Neem is known for its dense canopy of pinnate leaves and its ability to thrive in arid, drought-prone regions

Treatment for Diseases:

Diseases & its description

Skin Disorders

Various skin conditions including acne and eczema

Malaria

Parasitic Disease transmitted by mosquitoes

Part Name & its description

Leaf

Dark green, serrated leaves with a bitter taste

Bark

Brownish-grey bark with rough texture

Cure Method

Contains azadirachtin and other compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help treat and soothe skin disorders.

Bark contains compounds with antimalarial properties that help in combating malaria by interfering with parasite metabolism.
